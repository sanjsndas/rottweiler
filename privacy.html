Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Family's Trust, Naturally
Last Updated: June 1, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Train Your Rotty, we understand that trust is the foundation of every meaningful relationship - whether between human and dog, or between our training family and yours. Just as we honor the natural bond between you and your Rottweiler, we honor your right to privacy and protect your personal information with the same care we bring to training your beloved companion.

Our Promise: We collect only what we need to serve you and your Rottweiler better, we never sell your information, and we protect your data as carefully as we protect our own family.

Information We Collect

Training & Contact Information

To provide personalized training services for you and your Rottweiler, we collect:

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, and home address
  • Dog Information: Your Rottweiler's name, age, health status, behavioral history, and training goals
  • Training Records: Progress notes, session summaries, and behavioral observations
  • Emergency Contacts: Veterinarian information and emergency contact details
  • Payment Information: Billing address and payment method details (processed securely through our payment partners)

Website & Communication Data

When you visit our website or communicate with us, we may collect:

  • Website Usage: Pages visited, time spent, and general location (city/state level only)
  • Communication Records: Emails, messages, and phone call notes
  • Photos & Videos: Training progress documentation (only with your explicit consent)

How We Use Your Information

Training Services

  • Designing personalized training programs for your Rottweiler
  • Tracking progress and adjusting methods as needed
  • Communicating about sessions, schedules, and your dog's development
  • Providing ongoing support and guidance
  • Emergency situations involving your dog's safety

Service Improvement

  • Understanding what training methods work best
  • Developing new programs based on breed-specific needs
  • Improving our website and booking system
  • Creating educational content (always anonymized)

Communication

  • Sending appointment reminders and training tips
  • Sharing seasonal training advice and activities
  • Notifying you of new services or important updates
  • Responding to your questions and concerns

Information Sharing & Protection

We Never Sell Your Information. Your data is not a commodity to us - it's a trust you've placed in our care.

Limited Sharing Circumstances

We may share your information only in these specific situations:

  • Veterinary Emergencies: With your veterinarian if your dog requires immediate medical attention during training
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate (payment processors, scheduling systems) - all bound by strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect safety (we will notify you when legally permitted)
  • Business Transfer: If our business is sold, your information would transfer with appropriate protections

Data Security Measures

  • Encrypted data storage and transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access - only authorized team members
  • Secure payment processing through certified partners
  • Regular backup and recovery procedures

Your Privacy Rights

Access & Control

You have complete control over your information:

  • View: Request a copy of all information we have about you and your dog
  • Update: Correct or update any information at any time
  • Delete: Request deletion of your information (some training records may be retained for safety purposes)
  • Restrict: Limit how we use your information
  • Transfer: Receive your data in a portable format

Communication Preferences

You can control all communications from us:

  • Opt out of marketing emails while keeping essential training communications
  • Choose frequency of training tips and seasonal advice
  • Update your preferred communication methods
  • Unsubscribe from all non-essential communications

Cookies & Website Data

Our website uses minimal, essential cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Understand which content is most helpful to dog owners
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality but won't impact our training services.

Children's Privacy

Our services often involve families with children, and we take special care to protect young family members' privacy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you're a parent concerned about your child's information, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary:

  • Active Training: Throughout our training relationship and for ongoing support
  • Training Records: Up to 7 years for safety and reference purposes
  • Contact Information: Until you request deletion or unsubscribe
  • Website Data: 2 years for analytics and improvement purposes
  • Payment Records: As required by law and financial regulations

Updates to This Policy

Like a well-trained Rottweiler, this policy evolves with care and purpose. We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we do:

  • We'll notify you by email if changes significantly affect your rights
  • We'll post the updated policy on our website with a new "Last Updated" date
  • We'll continue honoring your preferences under the previous policy until you have a chance to review changes
